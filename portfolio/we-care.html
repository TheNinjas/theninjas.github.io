We Care: Communication System Design

An exploration into the dynamic relationship between colors and emotions using psychology experiment.

The main objective of this project was to study the dynamics between colour and the emotional state of a person and its further implementation in the field of interaction media. You can check out the detailed research oriented approach here

Submission

Was a part of a publication submitted at the British Council for Undergraduate Research '14' at University of Nottingham.

Duration

August to October 2013

Project Tags

Psychology, User Research, Programming
