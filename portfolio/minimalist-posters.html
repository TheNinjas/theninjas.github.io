Minimalist Posters

A series of minimalist posters visually depicting the various types of Goverments.

A Government is the system by which a state or community is governed. In the case of its broad associative defination, government, it consists of legislators, administrators and arbitrators. It is the means by which a state policy is enforced as well as the method to determine the policy of the state.In this project, I have created minimal designs, which would represent the meaning or the essence of a particular form of Goverment in the best possible manner. Classifications are on the basis of Aristarchic attributes, Autocratic attributes, Monarchic attributes, Pejorative attribute, Oligarchic attributes and socio-economic attributes.

Monarchy

Rule by royalty; a system of governance where an individual who has inherited the role and expects to bequeath it to their heir.

Bankocracy

Rule by banks; a system of governance with excessive power or influence of banks and other financial authorities on public policy-making. It can also refer to a form of government where financial institutions rule society.

Autocracy

Rule by one individual, whose decisions are subject to neither external legal restraints nor regularized mechanisms of popular control

Kratocracy

Rule by the strong; a system of governance where those who are strong enough seize power through physical force, social maneuvering or political cunning.

Geniocracy

Rule by the intelligent; a system of governance where creativity, innovation, intelligence and wisdom are required for those who wish to govern.

Kakistocracy

Rule by the stupid; a system of governance where the worst or least-qualified citizens govern or dictate policies.

Capitalist

In a capitalist or free-market goverment, people own their own businesses and property and must buy services for private use, such as healthcare.

Democracy

Rule by a government chosen by election where most of the populace are enfranchised. The key distinction between a democracy and other forms of constitutional government is usually taken to be that the right to vote is not limited by a person's wealth or race

Technocracy

Rule by the educated or technical experts; a system of governance where people who are skilled or proficient govern in their respective areas of expertise in technology would be in control of all decision making.

Diarchy

Variant of monarchy; a system of government in which two individuals, the diarchs, are the heads of state. In most diarchies, the diarchs hold their position for life and pass the responsibilities and power of the position to their children or family when they die.

Fascism

Rule by leader base only. Focuses heavily on patriotism and national identity. They believe their nation is based on commitment to an organic national community where its citizens are united together as one people through a national identity.

Socialism

Socialism is an government system characterised by social ownership of the means of production and co-operative management of the economy.
