The Quill

Corporate Branding for a fictitious Media House

This project was done in my second year, in a duration of about two weeks. The result is a comprehensive corporate branding for a fictitious brand.Too much of thought went into coming up with a universal logo for the brand and finally the Quill + Bookmark amalgamation was chosen. Later on, complete corporate branding of the firm was done, based on the logotype created and the guidelines established.
